## Scientific interpretation disclaimer

The analyses in this repository are observational and should be interpreted as associations.

The results do not establish causality, temporal ordering, clinical diagnosis, policy effectiveness, or formal mediation unless explicitly stated and methodologically supported.

The TPB-compatible mechanism analysis is exploratory. It should not be interpreted as a formal Theory of Planned Behavior mediation model.
